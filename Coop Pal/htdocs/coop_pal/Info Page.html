About the App

Coop Pal is designed to assist poultry farmers in remotely monitoring and managing their chickens. By combining modern technology with traditional farming practices, the app enhances the coop environment and boosts productivity in daily operations.


Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring: Track essential environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, light levels, air quality, feed weight, and water levels to ensure optimal conditions for raising chickens.

Egg Detection: Powered by AI, check if an egg is present inside the coop.

Device Control: Remotely control key devices like fan and lighting from your smartphone, ensuring the perfect environment anytime, anywhere.

Live Surveillance: Access a live video feed to monitor your coop in real-time, no matter where you are.
